Exploring how local communities shape globalization

Globalization challenges traditions, disrupts communities, and inspires creative resistance. This space explores how young people—through art, activism, and digital tools—are shaping a new future. Dive into stories of resilience, discover movements, and take action.

Case Studies

Oaxaca, Mexico

Oaxaca’s youth collectives, spurred by the violent repression of 2006, utilized art and music to reclaim public spaces. Groups like Arte Jaguar and the anarcho-punk scene crafted “rebel aesthetics,” transforming mundane walls into vibrant terrains of resistance. This creative defiance challenges both local oppression and global capitalist influences.
